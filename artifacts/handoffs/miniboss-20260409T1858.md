## Key Discovery: 32-bit DMA Root Cause

The ioDrive2 has `consistent_dma_mask=32bit` but `streaming_dma_mask=64bit`. On dual-EPYC with 252GB RAM, scatter-gather DMA maps pages above 4GB. The card's DMA engine can't read from addresses >4GB.

**Fix:** `dma_set_mask(dev, DMA_BIT_MASK(32))` + `dma_set_coherent_mask(dev, DMA_BIT_MASK(32))` in `kfio_pci_set_dma_mask()`.

This applies to ALL ioDrive2/ioScale2 cards on EPYC/high-memory systems.
